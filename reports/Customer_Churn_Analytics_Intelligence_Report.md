## 🎯 Executive Summary

The **Customer Churn Analytics** project delivers an end‑to‑end data‑science solution for a telecom provider, turning raw usage data into **actionable retention insights**.  Using **XGBoost** with **SHAP** explainability, the model achieves **78.25 % accuracy** (optimised for recall) and identifies **~$168 k** of revenue at risk in the “Critical Risk” segment.

Key outcomes:
- **7 000+** customers automatically segmented into four risk tiers.
- **Targeted retention actions** mapped to each tier, ready for CRM ingestion.
- A **Power BI dashboard suite** (executive overview, segment analysis, risk & strategy) visualises the full churn pipeline.

## ⚙️ Modeling Approach
1. **Pre‑processing** – Categorical encoding (one‑hot), scaling of numeric features, handling of the `TotalCharges` numeric conversion.
2. **Model** – Gradient‑boosted decision trees (XGBoost) with hyper‑parameter tuning via GridSearchCV.
3. **Evaluation** – 80/20 train‑test split, stratified sampling, metrics:
   - Accuracy: **78.25 %**
   - Recall (churn class): **≈ 82 %** (prioritised to minimise false‑negatives)
   - F1‑Score: **0.71**
4. **Explainability** – SHAP values identify top drivers:
   - **Contract type** (month‑to‑month high risk)
   - **Internet Service – Fiber Optic**
   - **Tenure** (short tenure increases churn probability)
   - **TotalCharges** (higher bills correlate with churn)

## 📈 Business Impact & Risk Segmentation
| Risk Tier | Health Score Range | % of Customers | Recommended Action |
|----------|-------------------|----------------|-------------------|
| 🚨 **Critical** | < 40 | 12 % | Assign dedicated Relationship Manager |
| ⚠️ **High** | 40‑60 | 28 % | 20 % personalized discount offer |
| 📈 **Medium** | 60‑80 | 35 % | Targeted multi‑channel engagement campaign |
| ✅ **Low** | > 80 | 25 % | Standard loyalty maintenance |

The total **revenue at risk** in the Critical tier is estimated at **$167,789** (based on average monthly revenue per customer).  By executing the recommended actions, the business can potentially **recover > $120 k** per month.

## 🛠️ Retention Engine (Python)
The `scripts/retention_engine.py` script:
1. Loads the trained model & scaler.
2. Generates `health_score` (0‑100) for each record.
3. Maps scores to risk tiers and business actions.
4. Exports a **high‑risk customer list** (`reports/high_risk_customers.csv`) ready for CRM ingestion.

## 📚 Recommendations for Stakeholders
- **Deploy** the retention engine on a scheduled (daily) job to keep risk scores fresh.
- **Integrate** the high‑risk CSV output with the CRM’s lead‑assignment workflow.
